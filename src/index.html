about |

:.who-am-i

My name's Emma. I was born in England, outside of London. I spent the majority of my childhood embroiled in a life of crime, but made a lucky escape when I moved away to study physics at Oxford University.
It was a shock to the system/change of culture coming to such a prestigious place, however Maths had always been a passion growing up and so I decided to apply that passion to the field of physics.
After graduating I managed to gain my first job in software engineering, working for a company named Ensoft, contracting exclusively for Cisco. Here I was fortunate enough to be trained inhouse in networking/software-engineering/networking protocols, in order to develop support for design features. Was there for four years before I bought a house in Harpenden near St Albans and decided to take a career break.
Renovated the house and was doing private tuition in maths/physics for 16-18yos. Moved to Australia 2017 to pursue a different lifestyle, had to work on a citrus farm for 3 months due to visa conditions.
My pursuit of re-training to become a web-developer is due to wanting to move into a different area of tech for greater opportunities !
